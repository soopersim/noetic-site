Use this file as the design reference for the Noetic landing page.

Direction:
- Premium, minimal, thoughtful, and editorial.
- Calm product presentation over startup theatrics.
- Strong typography, measured spacing, restrained borders, soft neutrals.
- Motion should feel quiet and deliberate, never decorative for its own sake.

Avoid:
- Shiny AI gradients, neon glows, and fake futuristic dashboards.
- Robot illustrations, floating orbs, or generic "tech future" motifs.
- Overcrowded feature grids or exaggerated marketing claims.

Visual cues:
- Linear-inspired clarity with a warmer, more human palette.
- Dark ink text, off-white backgrounds, muted navy accents.
- Rounded geometry, thin separators, subtle glass surfaces.
